Designed for Developers

No heavy apps, no distractions. Just light-speed terminal audio.

🌊

Autonomous Play

Launch with a single word. Moodwave scans, recommends, and starts playing matching music instantly.

🐛

Codebase Mood Scan

Analyzes directory trees, languages, TODO comments, and git markers to calculate your cognitive load and mood.

⚡

Zero Dependencies

Runs as a standalone binary. Leverages system audio pipelines (mpv/ffplay) to steam without installing Go.

🎨

Interactive Themes

Beautiful terminal visualizers featuring waveforms, spectrum analyzers, and 6 custom color schemes.

🔄

Self-Updating

Checks for updates and updates the binary in-place, keeping you running the latest version automatically.

🔍

Instant YouTube Search

Search and queue songs or live-streams on-the-fly directly inside the player console.

How Moodwave Analyzes Your Mood

01

File & Language Scan

Scans files and directory depths. Highly structured repos indicate focused moods; rapid multi-language builds indicate sprint mode.

02

Comment Semantic Extraction

Extracts expressions in comments. A high density of "TODO", "BUG", "FIXME", or "HACK" triggers a Debugging or Intense profile.

03

Recommender Matching

Matches traits (BPM range, energy, mood tags) with streaming music. Boosts related artists and similar genres to maintain flow.
